SPARK'S BRANDING GUIDELINES


Having appealing branding plays a big part in brand attraction. That's why we strived to make the best branding possible. Our logo combines a lightning bolt and the letter S. Our color palette is made to be bold, simple and appealing with yellow as it's main color.

This page contains all Spark branding assets and headlines how to use them in the best way possible

HOW TO USE THE LOGO CORRECTLY


We recommend using only our color palette's white, yellow and dark colourings when placing the logo on a background.

We do not recommend using light colors with yellow as it can hurt recognition of our brand

The Spark logo must not be smaller than 20px digitally or 5mm in print
